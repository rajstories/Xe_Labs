# InfluenceOS Track

InfluenceOS is an XE Labs Build Sprint 2026 challenge to build a creator discovery and campaign intelligence platform for Instagram and YouTube influencer marketing.

The expected prototype includes creator search and filters, comparison, campaign creation, creator-specific tracking links, a clearly labelled real or simulated analytics dashboard, and AI-generated campaign insights. Teams must not collect private data, bypass logins, or violate platform rules.

Full challenge: https://xelabs.in/build-sprint/influenceos
