# VoiceCore Track

VoiceCore is an XE Labs Build Sprint 2026 challenge to build a Hindi and Hinglish Indian voice AI agent. The expected prototype includes microphone input, speech recognition, grounded knowledge-base answers, transcript display, natural voice output, recent-turn memory, and a clear fallback for unsupported questions.

The agent should not invent facts and should state its real language and accent coverage honestly.

Full challenge: https://xelabs.in/build-sprint/voicecore
