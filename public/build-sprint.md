# XE Labs Build Sprint 2026

XE Labs Build Sprint 2026 is a free, online AI product hackathon for student builders graduating in 2027 or 2028. Individuals and teams of up to three members may choose one published product track and build a working prototype.

Selected finalists may be considered for paid internship opportunities based on performance, role fit, and a separate interview. Participation or finalist status does not guarantee an internship.

## Official timeline (IST)

- Registration opens: 29 June 2026, 9:00 AM.
- Idea submission window: 1 July to 7 July 2026, closing at 11:00 AM.
- Registration deadline: 7 July 2026, 11:00 AM.
- Idea results and selected-team prototype portal: 10 July 2026, 8:00 AM.
- Prototype deadline: 21 July 2026, 11:59 PM.
- Top 12 finalist announcement: 28 July 2026. Four teams are selected from each track.
- Top 12 final presentations: 30 July 2026.
- Winner announcement: 31 July 2026.

Internship-related next steps will be shared later with relevant winners or selected finalists through the email address or contact details provided during registration.

## Tracks

- [InfluenceOS](https://xelabs.in/build-sprint/influenceos): creator discovery and campaign intelligence.
- [EditDNA](https://xelabs.in/build-sprint/editdna): AI video editing automation.
- [VoiceCore](https://xelabs.in/build-sprint/voicecore): Hindi and Hinglish Indian voice AI.
